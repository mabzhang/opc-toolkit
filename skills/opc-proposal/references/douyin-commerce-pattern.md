# Douyin Commerce Workflow Pattern

Use this generic module library for Douyin commerce, live commerce, store
operation, Qianchuan, product-card SEO, KOL/KOC, media, Campaign, and
ecommerce-first brand proposals. It describes reusable workflow patterns and
does not include confidential client materials or claim results from a
particular brand.

Do not treat live commerce as the default first priority. The confirmed cases happened to focus on live, but future tasks may focus on Campaign, product card, Qianchuan, KOL, media, store operation or another business block. Always route by the user's current request.

## Reusable Planning Principles

Strong Douyin business plans are data-led:
- Start with local category data, sheet by sheet.
- Compare 2024 vs 2025, then month by month.
- Translate data into business choices: launch timing, price strategy, channel role, product card keywords, Qianchuan content, KOL budget and monthly GMV.
- Tie every action to a category node: 618, Double 11, Double 12, Spring Festival, Mother's Day, seasonal use cases.
- Make brand marketing concrete through brand house, visual hammer, language nail, campaigns, KOL calendar and TVC storyboards.

Client-facing commercial proposals may additionally include:
- Product portfolio choice by platform.
- GMV split by platform, product and channel.
- KOL tiering with real examples and expected role.
- Service team, scope, fee and quotation.
- Case proof and operational capability.

## Dynamic Routing

Choose the primary skeleton by current business priority:

| User Focus | Front-Load These Sections | Deprioritize Unless Needed |
|---|---|---|
| 直播/自播 | Live room positioning, assortment, SOP, host scripts, scene design, Qianchuan and live metrics | Broad brand theory |
| 抖店/商品卡 | Keyword trend, title SEO, main image, detail page, product card traffic, store score, membership | TVC and large Campaign |
| 千川/短视频/图文 | Audience, hook, material matrix, script formats, test budget, placement logic, weekly content calendar | Long brand manifesto |
| 品牌 Campaign | Core insight, brand house, visual hammer/language nail, campaign calendar, KOL/media, TVC/storyboards | Operational store minutiae |
| KOL/媒介 | Audience, platform role, KOL tiers, selection criteria, budget, deliverables, risk filters, media phasing | Product-detail page unless conversion-linked |
| 年度/月度运营 | 12-month calendar, GMV/KPI split, budget, campaign nodes, channel tasks, review mechanism | Single-material detail unless needed |
| 竞品/行业研究 | Source-backed industry, competitor, price, audience and keyword analysis, then strategic implications | Full execution unless requested |

## Module Library For Full Plans

Use these modules as building blocks. Reorder them by user priority instead of always using this exact order.

1. 项目封面与口径
   - Project period, product focus, business scope and priority.
   - State the current primary business focus, such as live-first, campaign-first, store-first or KOL-first.

2. 数据来源与资料口径
   - List local files and sheet names.
   - Include a source matrix: local-only, web-only, local-led with web supplement, or web-led with local validation.
   - Mark which facts come from local data, which are web-supplemented, and which are assumptions.

3. 行业数据深度洞察
   - Market size and growth.
   - Monthly sales rhythm and seasonality.
   - Price band: annual 2024 vs 2025 and monthly ASP.
   - Consumer profile: annual 2024 vs 2025 trend.
   - Keyword trend migration: not only top words, but changing concerns and whitespace terms.
   - Strategic implications.

4. 竞品格局深度分析
   - Top brands, tiers, price map and source.
   - Direct competitors, alternative competitors and high-end benchmarks.
   - Competitive pressure and attackable gaps.

5. 产品力与行业对标
   - Product matrix, hero SKU, entry SKU and profit SKU.
   - Feature-to-keyword match.
   - Price strategy and value narrative.

6. 消费者洞察与情感内核
   - Functional layer, efficiency layer and emotional layer.
   - Translate product features into emotional reasons to buy.
   - Define emotional anchors such as 时间焦虑, 家的温暖, 子女给父母, 安全感, 成分信任, 功效焦虑.

7. 平台/渠道全局策略框架
   - Seed/content layer, brand layer and conversion layer.
   - Explain each business block's role in the funnel.
   - Define yearly or phase ambition when useful.

8. 重点业务方案
   - Place the user's top-priority block here.
   - For live: positioning, persona, assortment, SOP, scripts, scenes, Qianchuan and metrics.
   - For Campaign: big idea, campaign system, media/KOL, TVC, content assets and KPI.
   - For product card/store: title, keywords, main images, detail page, store traffic and conversion.
   - For Qianchuan/content: material types, hook library, scripts, budgets, tests and scaling rules.
   - For KOL/media: tiers, roles, examples/search directions, budgets, deliverables and risk filters.

9. 支撑业务方案
   - Add secondary blocks only if they support the primary objective.
   - Avoid expanding every block equally when the user asked for a focused task.

10. 12个月或阶段化执行计划
   - Month-by-month or phase-by-phase table.
   - Include industry GMV/ASP when available, target GMV/KPI, key action, budget and dependency.

11. Campaign 与 TVC, if relevant
   - Annual campaigns with time, big idea, industry node and KPI.
   - TVC full storyboard: shot, duration, scene, visual, copy, sound.
   - Expand high-potential emotional themes into Qianchuan, live room and KOL directions.

12. 内容矩阵与素材策略
   - Material type matrix and proportions.
   - Front-3-second hooks.
   - Target audience and placement logic.
   - Weekly content calendar when relevant.

13. 新号冷启动/起号/启动方案, if relevant
   - Pre-launch checklist.
   - First month week-by-week plan.
   - Traffic mix and health metrics.
   - Common failure modes and fixes.

14. 预算、KPI 与任务单
   - Budget by business block, stage and purpose.
   - KPI by funnel layer.
   - Downstream task order with owner role, input, output, acceptance criteria and deadline.

15. 风险识别与应对
   - Brand cognition, price acceptance, category oligopoly, low-price attack, policy, inventory, quality and team risks.

16. 数据来源索引与免责声明
   - Data source table.
   - Calculated/inferred claims table.
   - Web search note when web results are unavailable, not authorized or only supplementary.

17. 服务与报价, optional
   - Use when the output is a client-facing pitch or business proposal.
   - Include service scope, team roles, fee/commission/ROI model and monthly sales split.

## Non-Negotiable Depth Checks

- Business priority must come from the current user request, not prior cases.
- Price analysis must include annual comparison and monthly high-ASP nodes when data exists.
- Consumer analysis must include annual trend, not only one period, when data exists.
- Keyword analysis must show migration and product whitespace.
- Product card titles must be verified against keyword trends when product card/store is in scope.
- Live room background must include green-screen and real-set options with test plan when live is in scope.
- Brand marketing must include brand house, visual/language assets, campaigns, KOL and TVC when brand Campaign is in scope.
- Month-by-month actions must match industry nodes and data.
- KOL budgets and Qianchuan budgets must be tied to monthly objectives when those blocks are in scope.
- TVC must have storyboard-level detail when requested.
- Data source index must explain where competitor and trend claims come from.
